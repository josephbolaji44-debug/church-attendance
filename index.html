📖 About Us

Welcome to The Sacred Cherubim & Seraphim Church of Nigeria, Oke Ayo Osu Model Parish, a place of worship, prayer, holiness, and divine encounter. We are a Christ-centered church committed to proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurturing spiritual growth, and leading souls into a deeper relationship with God.

Guided by the power of the Holy Spirit and the teachings of the Holy Bible, we uphold the values of faith, righteousness, love, and service. Through fervent prayers, inspiring worship, biblical teachings, and community outreach, we strive to bring hope, healing, and transformation to individuals, families, and society.

Our mission is to raise disciples of Christ, win souls for God's Kingdom, and create an atmosphere where everyone can experience God's presence and power. We welcome all people regardless of background to join us in worship as one family united in faith and love.

"A place where prayers are answered, lives are transformed, and God is glorified."
